Patch orientation

  1. Route sequencer or looper to sampler for a rhythm loop.
  2. Add life, raindrops, bounce, or dla to a synth.
  3. Use probability on sequencer cells so patterns feel alive.
  4. Bind aux encoders to parameters or actions you keep reaching for.

Factory preset hint: life on Part 1, sequencer rhythm on Part 2.

If the grid behaves unexpectedly, check the OLED. You are probably in an overlay or Dance page. Use Back or Fn to leave.
